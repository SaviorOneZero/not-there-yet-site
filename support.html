Not There Yet Support

Help and support for the Not There Yet iOS app.

Not There Yet is a family road-trip game for local, shared-device play. Add your road crew, start a trip, and play simple games together along the way.

Contact Support

For support, questions, bug reports, or feedback, email us at questplatformapps@gmail.com.

When contacting support, please include:

  • Your device model
  • Your iOS version
  • The app version and build number, if available
  • A short description of the issue or feedback
  • Steps to reproduce the issue, if you are reporting a bug

Sending Feedback from the App

Not There Yet may include a Send Feedback option in Settings. This uses the native iOS share sheet so you can send feedback through Mail, Messages, or another available sharing option on your device.

No Account Required

Not There Yet does not require an account, login, or subscription. Gameplay is designed for local use on a shared device.
