FAQ

Quick answers before the next drive.

Not There Yet keeps family play simple: six games, one shared device, no account required, and offline play wherever the road takes you.

Frequently asked questions

What is Not There Yet?

Not There Yet is a collection of six family road-trip games with quick things to spot, call out, and play along the way.

Which games are included?

License Plate Challenge, Alphabet Hunt, Vehicle Bingo, Roadside Oddities, Road Chain, and Roadside Challenge are all included.

Which devices are supported?

Not There Yet is designed for the whole family to play together on one shared device—an iPhone or iPad.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Every game works without an internet connection. Your Road Crew, Adventure progress, and scores stay on your device.

Does the app collect personal data?

No. Not There Yet does not use analytics, ads, tracking, accounts, location collection, or remote configuration.

Are there ads or subscriptions?

No. There are no ads and no subscriptions.

Are Road Crew names or Adventure Log details sent anywhere?

No. Road Crew names, Adventure progress, player stats, and Adventure Log details stay on the device.

Does everyone need their own device?

No. Everyone riding along can play together on one shared device.

Is it for kids?

It is made for family road trips and mixed ages. A parent or adult should still decide what fits their family, especially while traveling.

Can I send feedback?

Yes. Email questplatformapps@gmail.com. Nothing is sent automatically. We only receive feedback when you choose to email us.

Is Not There Yet affiliated with brands mentioned in gameplay prompts?

Not There Yet may mention common real-world vehicles, roadside objects, or delivery trucks in spotting prompts. These references are for gameplay only. Not There Yet is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any third-party brand mentioned in the app.

Will there be more games in the future?

More games and content may be added in future updates.
