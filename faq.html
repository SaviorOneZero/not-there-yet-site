FAQ

Quick answers before the next drive.

Not There Yet is meant to be simple: six games, one shared device, offline play, and no accounts or extra setup in the car.

Frequently asked questions

What is Not There Yet?

Not There Yet is a collection of six family road-trip games with quick things to spot, call out, and play along the way.

Which games are included?

Roadside Challenge, License Plate Challenge, Alphabet Hunt, Vehicle Bingo, Roadside Oddities, and Road Chain are all included.

Which devices are supported?

Not There Yet is designed for shared-device play on iPhone and iPad.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Not There Yet is local-only and does not rely on accounts, analytics, ads, tracking, remote configuration, or a server connection for gameplay.

Does the app collect personal data?

No. Not There Yet does not use analytics, ads, tracking, accounts, location collection, or remote configuration.

Are there ads or subscriptions?

No. Not There Yet has no ads and no subscriptions.

Are road crew names or trip history sent anywhere?

No. Road crew names, game progress, player stats, and trip history stay on the device.

Is this a one-device or multi-device game?

It is a shared-device game. One device can be used by everyone riding along.

Is it for kids?

It is made for family road trips and mixed ages. A parent or adult should still decide what fits their family, especially while traveling.

Can I send feedback?

Yes. Email questplatformapps@gmail.com. Nothing is sent automatically. We only receive feedback when you choose to email us.

Is Not There Yet affiliated with brands mentioned in gameplay prompts?

Not There Yet may include plain-text references to common real-world vehicles, roadside objects, or delivery trucks as road-trip spotting prompts. These references are for gameplay only. Not There Yet is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any third-party brand mentioned in the app.

Will there be more games in the future?

More games and content may be added in future updates.
