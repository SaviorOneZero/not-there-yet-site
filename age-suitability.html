Age Suitability

Made for families and mixed ages.

Not There Yet is designed for families and mixed-age groups to play together on one shared device.

A simple, family-friendly experience

Not There Yet contains no advertising, messaging, user-generated content, unrestricted web access, purchases, or objectionable material. The app works offline, requires no account, and does not collect location data.

For parents and guardians

Parents and guardians should decide what is appropriate for their family and supervise device use while traveling.
