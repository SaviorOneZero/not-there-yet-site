About Not There Yet

Made for the miles between leaving and finally getting there.

Not There Yet is a Quest Platform app built around a simple idea: road trips are better when everyone has a reason to look up. Six included games turn the drive into a light, local-only adventure of spotting things, calling them out, and noticing the world outside.

What it is

It's a casual shared-device road-trip game for families. Play a quick round when the car gets quiet, keep something going until the next stop, or use it as an excuse to notice the strange signs and tiny details along the way. One iPhone or iPad is all the crew needs, and every game works offline.

What we care about

  • Easy enough to start while the car is already moving
  • Good for mixed ages, short attention spans, and real family trips
  • Six included games with no accounts, ads, subscriptions, or tracking
