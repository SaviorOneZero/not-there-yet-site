About Not There Yet

Built for the miles between leaving and arriving.

Not There Yet started with a simple idea: make road trips feel more playful, more shared, and easier to enjoy. The experience is designed to spark conversation, encourage observation, and turn ordinary travel time into something memorable.

What the experience is about

It is a light, modern app for families who want a fun, low-friction way to make travel time feel more engaging. Whether the goal is a quick round of a road-trip game or a shared challenge that lasts the whole drive, the experience stays easy to understand and pleasant to use.

Design principles

  • Fun, family-friendly, and easy to pick up
  • Modern, readable, and mobile-friendly
  • Built to grow with future expansion packs and updates
