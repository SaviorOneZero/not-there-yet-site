About Not There Yet

Made for the miles between leaving and finally getting there.

Not There Yet is built around a simple idea: road trips are better when everyone has a reason to look up. Six included games turn every drive into an adventure built around spotting things, calling them out, and noticing the world outside.

How families play

Choose a game, set up your Road Crew, and share one iPhone or iPad. Play a quick round when the car gets quiet, keep an Adventure going until the next stop, or simply notice more along the way. Every game works offline.

What we care about

  • Easy enough to start while the car is already moving
  • Good for mixed ages, short attention spans, and real family trips
  • Six included games with no account required, no ads, no subscriptions, and no tracking
