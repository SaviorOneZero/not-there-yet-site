About Not There Yet

Made for the miles between leaving and getting there.

Not There Yet is a Quest Platform app from founder Andrew Reynolds and a small product team. It started with a simple thought: road trips are better when everyone has a reason to look up. The app turns the drive into a light game of spotting, calling out, and noticing the world outside.

What it is

It is a casual road-trip game for families. Play a quick round when the car gets quiet, keep a challenge going until the next stop, or use it as an excuse to notice the strange signs and tiny details along the way.

What we care about

  • Easy enough to start while the car is already moving
  • Friendly for mixed ages, short attention spans, and real family trips
  • Simple today, with room for more games and themes later
