About Not There Yet

Built for the miles between leaving and arriving.

Not There Yet began with a simple idea: make road trips feel more playful, more shared, and more enjoyable. It is designed to spark conversation, encourage observation, and turn ordinary travel time into something memorable.

What the experience is about

It is a light, modern app for families who want a fun, low-friction way to make travel time feel more engaging. Whether it is a quick lookout game or a shared challenge that lasts until the next stop, the experience stays simple, easy to understand, and enjoyable to use.

Design principles

  • Fun, family-friendly, and easy to pick up
  • Modern, readable, and mobile-friendly
  • Built to grow with future expansion packs and updates
