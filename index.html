Not There Yet

A family road-trip game for the miles between here and there.

Play six quick games together on one shared iPhone or iPad. Spot plates, signs, vehicles, and roadside surprises that give everyone a reason to look up, call something out, and join in.

See how it works
Next checkpoint
  • Look outside Hunt for license plates, letters, vehicles, signs, and odd little things along the road.
  • Share one device Pass the phone around or let one person keep score while everyone calls out what they see.
  • No signal needed Every game works offline. No accounts, ads, subscriptions, analytics, or tracking.

What's inside

Six ways to play the road.

License Plate Challenge

Spot license plates from all 50 states.

Alphabet Hunt

Find every letter from A to Z on signs, vehicles, and license plates.

Vehicle Bingo

Look for cars, trucks, buses, and other vehicles along the way.

Roadside Oddities

Discover giant objects, unusual landmarks, funny signs, and other roadside surprises.

Road Chain

Take turns finding something that starts with the last letter of the previous answer.

New in v1.3

Roadside Challenge

Take turns drawing roadside spotting prompts and race to the target score.

Keep the same Road Crew for the whole trip

Start with one game or keep an Adventure going across several. Not There Yet records completed games, winners, and scores in the Adventure Log, all on your device.

No account. Your trip stays on your device.

Road Crew names, scores, and Adventure details stay on your iPhone or iPad.
