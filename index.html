Not There Yet

A family road-trip game for the miles between here and there.

Play six quick games together on one shared device. Spot plates, signs, vehicles, roadside surprises, and all the little details that make a long drive feel shorter.

How it works
Next checkpoint
  • Look outside Find license plates, letters, vehicles, signs, and odd little things along the road.
  • Share one device Pass the phone around, call things out, and keep everyone riding along in the same game.
  • Play anywhere No account required. No ads. No subscriptions. No analytics or tracking. Works offline.

What's inside

Classic road-trip games, made for quick play.

License Plate Challenge

Spot license plates from all 50 states.

Alphabet Hunt

Find every letter from A to Z on signs, vehicles, and license plates.

Vehicle Bingo

Look for cars, trucks, buses, and other vehicles along the way.

Roadside Oddities

Discover giant objects, unusual landmarks, funny signs, and other roadside surprises.

Road Chain

Take turns finding something that starts with the last letter of the previous answer.

New in v1.3

Roadside Challenge

Complete fun roadside spotting challenges and race to the winning score.

One Adventure, multiple games

Add your Road Crew. Start an Adventure. Play a single game or keep your Road Crew together across a full Adventure.

Private by design

Not There Yet remembers completed games, player stats, winners, and your Adventure Log so every road trip leaves a little story behind.
