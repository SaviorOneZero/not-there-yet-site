Release Notes

What's new in Not There Yet.

A simple record of updates, new games, and improvements to Not There Yet.

v1.3

Take on the Roadside Challenge

Roadside Challenge is a new turn-based spotting game where each player gets a prompt, everyone helps look, and the active player scores. The first player to the target score wins. Adventures, onboarding, and gameplay actions have also been polished to make it easier for the whole crew to get started and keep playing.

  • New turn-based Roadside Challenge game
  • 128 prompts in the included Classic Road Trip deck
  • Adjustable target score
  • Saved progress when an adventure is interrupted
  • Final scoreboards and tie handling
  • More consistent primary action buttons

v1.2

A smoother adventure

This update makes Not There Yet more comfortable on iPad and easier to follow throughout an adventure. Completed games are now organized in the Adventure Log, Road Chain decisions are clearer, and game summaries have been polished.

  • Improved layouts for iPad
  • Renamed Trip History to Adventure Log
  • Grouped completed games by trip, crew, and day
  • Added clearer Road Chain elimination and end-game confirmations
  • Refined winner and trip-summary wording

v1.1

Road-tested update

Road-tested update with Roadside Oddities, expanded spotting prompts, gameplay copy polish, and small usability improvements based on early feedback.

v1.0

Initial App Store release

Initial App Store release of Not There Yet, including road crew setup, road-trip games, active adventure flow, trip history, player stats, Settings, Support, and Privacy information.
