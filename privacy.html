Privacy Policy

Privacy, kept simple.

Not There Yet is a local-only app. This page explains what stays on your device and what happens if you choose to email support.

What the app does not collect

  • No analytics
  • No ads
  • No tracking
  • No accounts
  • No location collection
  • No remote configuration

What stays on your device

Road crew names, game progress, player stats, and trip history are stored locally on your device. They are not sent to Quest Platform by the app.

Support email

If you email support, we receive whatever details you choose to send. That email is used to answer your question, troubleshoot an issue, or review feedback.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, please contact questplatformapps@gmail.com.
