Privacy Policy

Not There Yet respects your privacy and keeps the experience simple.

This policy explains what information is collected by Not There Yet, how it is used, and how you can reach out with questions.

What we collect

Not There Yet is designed to be lightweight. We do not collect personal information through this website. If the app collects usage information, it is limited to basic diagnostics and app activity used to understand performance and improve the experience. If you email support, we receive the details you choose to share.

How it is used

Information shared with us is used to improve Not There Yet, respond to support requests, troubleshoot issues, and maintain the quality of the product. We do not sell personal information.
