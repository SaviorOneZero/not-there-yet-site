Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Not There Yet handles information. Not There Yet is a family road-trip game for iOS built for local, shared-device play.

Information We Collect

Not There Yet does not require an account, does not require login, and does not collect personal information from users.

The app does not use third-party advertising, tracking, analytics, or marketing SDKs.

Information Stored on Your Device

Not There Yet may store gameplay information locally on your device, such as player names, trip history, game results, and player stats. This information is used only to support the app’s gameplay and history features.

This local gameplay information is stored on your device and is not sent to us.

Feedback

Feedback is only sent if you choose to send it. The message may include basic app and device information such as the app version, build number, device type, and iOS version to help with troubleshooting.

Feedback is sent to questplatformapps@gmail.com when you send the email, or through another sharing option if fallback sharing is used. The app does not send feedback automatically.

Children’s Privacy

Not There Yet is intended for family use and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not include accounts, social features, advertising, or tracking.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. The app does not collect personal information for advertising or tracking purposes.

Data Deletion

Because gameplay information is stored locally on your device, you can remove it by deleting the app from your device. Future versions of the app may include additional in-app controls for clearing local gameplay history.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at questplatformapps@gmail.com.
